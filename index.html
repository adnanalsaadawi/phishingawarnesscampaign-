What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of online scam where attackers impersonate legitimate organizations or individuals to steal sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details.

How to Prevent Phishing Attacks:

  • Be cautious with emails: Never click on links in unsolicited emails or messages.
  • Check the URL: Verify the website's URL before entering sensitive information.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA): Always use 2FA for added security.
  • Report suspicious activity: Report any phishing attempts to your email provider or security team.
